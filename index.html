My Love,

In the journey of life, I find myself often the one who ignites the storms. But you—you are the most extraordinary soul I know, the embodiment of everything good I’ve ever done. You are the fruit of every virtuous action I’ve taken, and for that, I am forever grateful.

My heart aches with regret for the moments when my anger has cast a shadow over your light. It is a pain I never wish to inflict again. I long for your forgiveness and a chance to make things right, not just today, but every day.

To me, love isn’t confined to special occasions—it’s in every heartbeat, every glance, every word shared. Valentine’s Day is but a reminder to cherish you, but in my world, every day is an opportunity to celebrate the love we share.

Happy Valentine’s Day, my love. You mean the world to me, now and always.
